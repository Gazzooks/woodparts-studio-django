Hardwood Lumber
Unlike dimensional lumber, hardwoods are sold by the board foot, a unit of volume equivalent to a board that is 1" thick, 12" wide, and 12" long. Hardwoods are typically sold in specific thickness classifications:
Nominal Thickness Actual Thickness (inches) Actual Thickness (mm)
4/4 (Four quarters)13/16" – 7/8"20.6mm – 22.2mm
5/4 (Five quarters)1-1/16" – 1-1/8"27.0mm – 28.6mm
6/4 (Six quarters)1-5/16" – 1-3/8"33.3mm – 34.9mm
8/4 (Eight quarters)1-13/16" – 1-7/8"46.0mm – 47.6mm
12/4 (Twelve quarters)2-13/16" – 2-7/8"71.4mm – 73.0mm
16/4 (Sixteen quarters)3-13/16" – 3-7/8"96.8mm – 98.4mm
Note:
Hardwood actual thicknesses can vary based on final surfacing. Rough sawn lumber will be slightly thicker than the final surfaced dimensions shown here. Width and length are typically measured in inches and are exact measurements.
